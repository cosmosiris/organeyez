Setting Up Your Organ Eyez Network

Organization Account

A: In order for Organ Eyez to work for you, you should have the following:

  • Have an established organization with an active group of working members
  • Have a way to determine the difference between "active members" of your organization and "supporters". Only "active members" will have full access to everything Organ Eyez has to offer
  • Establish membership expectations
  • Establish voting requirements and processes

B: Setup your Account

  1. Create an organizational admin account here

  2. Design the profile page of your admin account by filling out the following sections:
    • mission statement
    • principles/values
    • contact information
    • Resources (ex: documents, referrals, website links, orientation packets, etc)
    • Profile picture

  3. Select your organization's membership expectations and metrics. Example membership expactions include:
    • Attendance at 2/3 of general body meetings in the last 6 months
    • Participate on a planning committee for at least 2 events in the last 6 months
    • Attendance at minimum of 1 training in the last 6 months
    • Completion of at least 2 one on one's in the last 6 months

  4. Upload a list of members using an excel spreadsheet. The list must include each member's first name, last name, and email address

  5. You will receive a link to invite your members to create an account. Each member will recieve a specific code that they must use to register

Member Accounts

A: In order for Organ Eyez to work for you, you must meet the following:

  • You must be an "active member" of an organization that is registered on the Organ Eyez Network
  • You must receive an email invitation to setup your Organ Eyez member account. This email should contain a 7-digit code that you will use to sign-up
  • If you are not able to register an account with us it means that your organization did not submit your information, or the information they submitted is incorrect.

B: Setup your Account

  1. Create your member account here

  2. Design your profile page by filling out the following sections:
    • Basic info
    • Date you joined your organization
    • Leadership positions
    • Contact information
    • Profile Picture

  3. From here you should be able to do the following:
    • View your organization's profile page
    • View the profile pages of every member within your organization
    • Post status updates
    • Setup a one on one meeting
    • View your calendar and integrate it into your google calendar
    • Create an event and upload attendance sheets, or meeting minutes
    • Private message fellow members
    • Create a group chat
    • Create a poll
    • Request an accountability process
    • Create a project
    • Comment on other member's pages
