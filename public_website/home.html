The social media and accountability tool for social justice organizations.

Organ Eyez enhances productivity, communication, and accountability for activists and organizers giving you a better shot at changing your community, and ultimately, changing the world.

Who is this app for?

  • Community organizations and social justice groups, especially those with horizontal leadership structures

What does it do?

  • Provides a private social media platform where members can share status updates and internal organizational communications
  • Facilitates transparent accountability processes
  • Displays membership expectations and each member's progress towards fulfilling their membership expectations
  • Tracks membership participation and attendance
  • Provides a voting platform for both large-scale voting and small-scale polling

Why should I use it?

  • Transparency increases trust and mutual understanding
  • All members can see who is meeting the membership expectations. Members can more easily hold each other accountable to the organization's expectations as well as identify members who need additional support
  • Faster growth in membership of dedicated individuals.
  • Greater committment, more follow-through, and ultimately more productivity
  • Decisions can be made faster and communications are all stored in one central place
  • All of these factors will help your organization to progress faster than ever before
