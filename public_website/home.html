An organizing tool for membership and volunteer based organizations

Who is this app for?

  • Small non-profit organizations
  • Small social justic groups
  • Membership based organizations
  • Clubs
  • Associations
  • Community Organizations

What does it do?

  • Creates organizational transparency
  • Tracks membership participation
  • Visualizes project tasks and deadlines
  • Facilitates voting
  • Facilitates quick and easy communication
  • Facilitates urgent decision-making

Why should I use it?

  • Transparency increases trust and mutual understanding
  • All members can see who is meeting the membership expectations. Members can more easily hold each other accountable to the organization's expectations as well as identify members who need additional support
  • Faster growth in membership of dedicated individuals.
  • Greater committment, more follow-through, and ultimately more productivity
  • Decisions can be made faster and communications are all stored in one central place
  • All of these factors will help your organization to progress faster than ever before
